About Me

I'm Laura, a former public school teacher with over ten years experience in the classroom. I am super passionate about helping others and want to create positive experiences for people through design and technology that solves real-world needs.

I started my journey into programming taking night courses at my local community college. After learning the basics and succesfully designing and prototyping two mobile applications, I enrolled in a full-time coding program in Tokyo, Japan.

Outside of improving my development skills, I enjoy running, reading, tending to my houseplants, and of course, eating lots of Spam for breakfast.

Why the Spam?

It's a nod to my Japanese-Hawaiian heritage and a big part of my childhood. And because it's delicious. If you've never tried it here's the skinny. Fry a few eggs, throw them over a bed of steamed rice, toss in 2 slices of pan-fried Spam then top this beautiful mess with ketchup and you will be on your way to a Hawaiian breakfast paradise.
